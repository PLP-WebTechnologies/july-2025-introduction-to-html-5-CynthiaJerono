What is Digital Accessibility?

Digital accessibility refers to the practice of making digital content usable by people with various disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

Why It's Important

Accessibility ensures that everyone can access information and services online, promoting equality and inclusion. It also enhances user experience for all users, not just those with disabilities.

Accessibility Guidelines

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) provide a framework for making web content more accessible. They include principles such as perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust.

How to Improve Accessibility

  • Use semantic HTML elements.
  • Provide alternative text for images.
  • Ensure sufficient color contrast.
  • Make navigation keyboard-friendly.
  • Test your website with assistive technologies.
